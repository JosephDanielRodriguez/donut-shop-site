About Us

Joe and Hillary Johnson have been in business for nearly 20 years. Joe has been working with donuts from a very early age when his father owned the shop. He would always come home after school and go straight to work helping his father knead the dough among many other minor roles in the shop. Hillary went to culinary school where she learned about the art of confectionaries and was top of her class. After the passing of Joe's father, he inherited the donut shop. The two met at the very donut shop in which they run now and have been working there ever since.
